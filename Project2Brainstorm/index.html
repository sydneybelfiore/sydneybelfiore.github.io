BRAINSTORM

We're creating a website where users can learn about and debate cats and dogs. We'll include homepages for info about both cats and dogs, a gallery, about us, and of course... a forum. Style-wise, our primary colors are dark blue and yellow, with our secondary colors being light blue and orange. We'll have medium padded borders, soft edges, no shadow boxes, playful font, medium-sized font, and of course plenty of images.
